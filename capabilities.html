Our CNC Capabilities

Mantriq continually invests in the latest CNC machinery, including CNC turning centers, multi-axis milling machines, and automated production systems.

CNC Turning Capabilities:

  • Components from 2mm to 300mm diameter
  • Live tooling and complex geometries
  • High-volume production to single prototypes
  • Advanced bar feeding technology
  • 24/7 unmanned production capabilities

CNC Milling Capabilities:

  • Components up to 1000mm in size
  • 3, 4, and 5-axis machining
  • High-speed production systems
  • Precision drilling and tapping
  • Complex geometries and tight tolerances
