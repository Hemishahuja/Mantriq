About us

LEADING UK SUPPLIERS OF HIGH-QUALITY PRECISION TURNED & MILLED COMPONENTS.

Welcome Northern Cam

Since 1977, Northern Cam has been providing a high-quality precision engineering service to a diverse range of industries throughout the world.

Many of these industries require specialist components that perform in demanding environments.

Today, as then, we are still a family-owned business. Our commitment to investing in the best technology, manufacturing quality products, employing knowledgeable people and insisting on high levels of customer service is in our opinion the four pillars to our success. 

1977

The founding of Northern Cam

Founded by Pritpal Singh Bhabra in 1977, Northern Cam began providing their high-quality precision engineering service, based in the Leeds area. Pritpal Singh Bhabra completed his engineering degree at Leeds University in 1963 and went on to become one of the first Engineering Draftsman of Indian decent in Yorkshire at his employers.  When the opportunity of purchasing a single CAM Auto Machine was presented to Pritpal Bhabra, he gathered all his saving and set up a new company in a small workshop and named it Northern Cam   Pritpal managed the company throughout the years and Northern Cam continued to expand rapidly and successfully. 

1977

Becoming a limited company

In 1977, Northern Cam was officialy incorporated with Pritpal Bhabra as Managing Director.

1992

Pritpal Singh Bhabra Quality Assurance Auditor Training

Pritpal Singh Bhabra completed his Quality Assurance Auditor training 14th -15th October 1992. 

1994

A family business!

Pritpals sons Satvinder Singh Bhabra and Parvinder Singh Bhabra joined Northern Cam, truly became a family business together. 

1998

Northern Cams international growth

As a result of the rapid growth, Northern Cam began to grow in size with the creation of another manufacturing base in India and an established, strong and reliable supply chain from China, which was/is incredibly unique for a UK manufacturing supplier to have such intrinsic links. 

2020

Attending the Southern Manufacturing show

Northern Cam attends the Southern Manufacturing show for the first time!

2023

Pritpal Singh Bhabra's passing

We were deeply saddened by the passing of our founder and managing director, Pritpal Singh Bhabra. His absence was profoundly felt, and we remained committed to honouring his legacy. Satvinder Bhabra took on the role of Managing Director, and Parvinder Bhabra stepped into the role of Finance Director. 

2024

Present day Northern Cam

Today, as then, we are still a family-owned business. Our commitment to investing in the best technology, manufacturing quality products, employing knowledgeable people and insisting on high levels of customer service is in our opinion the four pillars to our ongoing success.
