About Mantriq

Mantriq has been providing high-quality precision CNC machining services to Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area (GTA).

We specialize in manufacturing components that perform in demanding environments across various industries. Our commitment to investing in the latest CNC technology, maintaining the highest quality standards, employing skilled professionals, and delivering exceptional customer service sets us apart in the Toronto manufacturing landscape.

As a Toronto-based precision machining company, we understand the unique needs of Canadian manufacturers and provide solutions that meet both local and international standards.
